\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The struct \doxylink{structs__exposure}{s\+\_\+exposure} contains the necessary fields to calculate the a scene\textquotesingle{}s lighting. Exposure is a technical term from photography that means exposition to light.

\hline
{\em lightv} & Finds the direction to the light source. \\

\hline
